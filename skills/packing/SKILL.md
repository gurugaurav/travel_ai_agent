description: Generates a smart packing list tailored to the destination, season, and activities
---

When asked for a packing list, generate a concise categorized list:
- **Clothing** — based on climate (beach/humid/cold) and planned activities
- **Essentials** — documents (ID, tickets), money (cash + card), medications, sunscreen
- **Electronics & Accessories** — phone, charger, power bank, earphones
- **Activity-specific gear** — beach kit, hiking shoes, etc. only if relevant

Keep it practical — no bloat, no obvious everyday items everyone already packs.
Add a "Pro Tips" section at the end with 2-3 destination-specific travel hacks.

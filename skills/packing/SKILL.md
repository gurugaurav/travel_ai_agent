When asked for a packing list, use all available context — destination, weather summary, planned activities, duration, and any packing preferences from memory (carry-on only, minimalist, etc.).

## Rules
- No obvious everyday items (toothbrush, soap — skip them)
- Be specific to the destination: beach trip ≠ hill station ≠ city break
- If weather has rain risk, include rain gear
- If carry-on only, flag items that must be under 100ml and suggest packing cubes
- Keep total under 40 items
- Clothing count should match trip duration realistically (don't list 7 outfits for a 4-day trip)

## Format

## Packing List — [Destination] ([N] days)

### 👕 Clothing
- Items based on weather, activities, and duration

### 🧴 Essentials
- Documents (ID, flight tickets, travel insurance)
- Destination-specific items (e.g. visa printout, vaccination certificate)

### 📱 Electronics & Accessories
- Only what's relevant — skip the obvious

### 🏖️ Activity Gear
- Destination and activity specific only (beach kit, trekking poles, etc.)

### 💊 Health & Safety
- Destination-specific: mosquito repellent for Goa, altitude sickness pills for Leh, etc.

### 💡 Pro Tips
- 2–3 destination-specific packing hacks (e.g. "Buy a cheap rain poncho at the Goa market — don't pack one from home")
